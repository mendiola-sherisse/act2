The Heart of the Filipino

Philippine Airlines (PAL) is the flag carrier of the Philippines, offering world-class service with genuine Filipino warmth since 1941.
As Asia's first commercial airline, we've been connecting the Philippines to the world for over 80 years.

BOOK A FLIGHT

PAL flies to over 30 domestic destinations and 40 international cities across 4 continents, with our hub at Ninoy Aquino International Airport.

Our Legacy

Founded on February 26, 1941, Philippine Airlines is the oldest commercial airline in Asia operating under its original name.

Global Network

We operate flights to major cities in North America, Asia, Australia, and the Middle East, with plans to expand our European routes.

Awards

Recognized as a 4-star airline by Skytrax and awarded "World's Most Improved Airline" in 2019.
